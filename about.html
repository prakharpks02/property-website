About FlatX

FlatX offers an AI-powered room rental platform tailored for tier 2 and tier 3 cities. Our services include direct tenant-to-landlord connections, eliminating broker fees and ensuring complete transparency. Explore rooms with our Video Vlog System for detailed insights before scheduling a visit. Enjoy an extensive listing of verified properties, simple booking processes, and a dedicated support team to guide you.

BENEFITS

  • Cost Savings: No hefty broker commissions.
  • Transparency: Direct interactions with landlords.
  • Variety: Wide range of verified room options to choose from.
  • Support: Dedicated assistance for a smooth renting experience.
  • Convenience: View rooms anytime with our Video Vlog System.
